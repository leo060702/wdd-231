Yosemite Travel Guide - Site Plan

Site Name

Yosemite Travel Guide

This name clearly represents the purpose of the website. Yosemite National Park is a world-renowned travel destination, and "Travel Guide" clarifies that the site provides essential travel information for visitors.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to offer comprehensive travel information for visitors to Yosemite National Park. It will include guides to major attractions, recommended hiking trails, lodging and camping options, transportation advice, and safety tips. The goal is to help users plan an enjoyable and safe trip to one of America's most iconic national parks.

Scenarios

  • What are the best hiking trails in Yosemite for beginners and families?
  • How can I reserve a campsite inside Yosemite National Park?
  • What is the best time of year to visit Yosemite to see the waterfalls?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #2E8B57 (Sea Green) - Navigation bar, main headings, and callouts
  • Secondary Color: #4682B4 (Steel Blue) - Buttons, links, and section headers
  • Accent Color: #FFD700 (Gold) - Call-to-action buttons and highlight sections
  • Background Color: #FFFFFF (White) - Overall background

Typography

  • Heading Font: Roboto Slab — for major headings and titles
  • Body Font: Open Sans — for general text
  • Special Text Font: Lato — for small labels and navigation links

All fonts will be imported via Google Fonts.

Wireframe

Desktop View

  • Top: Logo + horizontal navigation menu
  • Hero Section: Full-width banner image with introductory text and call-to-action button
  • Three-column Layout: Attractions | Trails | Lodging Info
  • Special Section: Reserve Campsite, Download Map
  • Footer: Contact information, newsletter signup, social media links

Page Overview

1. Home

  • Hero Image with welcome text
  • Brief introduction about Yosemite National Park
  • Featured attractions and quick links
  • Seasonal announcements or travel tips
  • Quick navigation buttons
  • Footer with contact and social media links

2. Attractions

  • List of key attractions with images and descriptions
  • Highlighted must-visit places
  • Links to detailed pages for each attraction

3. Hiking Trails

  • Trails categorized by difficulty (Beginner | Intermediate | Advanced)
  • Trail information: length, estimated time, difficulty, start/end points
  • Safety tips for hiking in Yosemite

4. Lodging & Camping

  • Lodging options (hotels, lodges inside the park)
  • Detailed camping sites information
  • Reservation tips and seasonal availability

5. Contact

  • Contact form (Name, Email, Message)
  • Official contact information (phone, email)
  • Social media links (Instagram, Facebook)

Technologies Used

  • HTML5: Semantic HTML structure with header, nav, main, footer
  • CSS3: External stylesheet, responsive design with media queries
  • Google Fonts: Roboto Slab, Open Sans, Lato
  • Responsive Web Design: Mobile-first approach
  • Accessibility Best Practices: Alt text, color contrast, semantic markup
  • SEO Basics: Proper title, meta description, heading structure
  • W3C Validation: HTML and CSS validation

Style Guide Summary

  • Color Palette: Green and blue tones with gold highlights
  • Typography: Clear, readable fonts; strong headlines; easy-to-read body text
  • Layout Style: Clean, simple, organized; intuitive navigation
  • Design Focus: Clear information hierarchy; strong visual appeal with nature imagery
